Who We Are

A Compassion-Led Tech Initiative for Animal Welfare

Cow Saver is a digital IT + NGO initiative working to protect stray cows and dogs through intelligent rescue response, awareness campaigns, and infrastructure support. From reflector belt drives to shelter construction and medical outreach, every project is designed to create long-term impact.

How We Work

  • Digital rescue alerts and volunteer coordination
  • Community awareness and public safety campaigns
  • Shelter and feeding infrastructure development
